Step 1: Problem

Identify challenges and design a problem statement

PHASE 1

1 Identifying Challenges (3–4) Using UN SDGs

利用联合国可持续发展目标，辨识出社会现有的挑战 (3–4)，并解释为什么是个挑战。

Using the UN SDGs as a lens, identify 3–4 challenges in the area you are focusing on. Explain why each is a challenge.

SDG Reference: SDG 1 No Poverty SDG 2 Zero Hunger SDG 3 Good Health SDG 4 Quality Education SDG 10 Reduced Inequalities SDG 11 Sustainable Cities SDG 13 Climate Action SDG 16 Peace & Justice

2 Select an Underlying Problem & Design a Problem Statement

从辨识出的挑战中选择并确定1个研究课题。设立1个问题陈述。

From the challenges above, select one underlying problem. Research it and design a Problem Statement using the template below.

📋 Problem Statement Template

Condition Statement: Because of [root cause / context]...
Stem + Key Verb Phrase: ...how might we [create / design / develop] [solution area] for [target group]...
Purpose: ...in order to [desired outcome / impact]...
Future Scene Parameters: ...in [location / context] in [year] and beyond?

Example:

"Because of ethical concerns caused by the new surgical procedure, how might we create a positive medical environment for XX treatments in order to encourage ethical use of XX surgery in the U.S. in 2023 and beyond?"

Step 2: Solution

Generate, evaluate and select the best solution

PHASE 2

1 Generate Solution Ideas

对比现有的解决方式，并提出 ≥3 个创新的解决方法。

Compare and explain existing measures or solutions in both cities. Then come up with at least 3 new innovative solutions.

2 Identify Criteria & Evaluate Solutions (3–4 Criteria)

设立标准 (3–4) 来评估自己提议的解决方法。

Identify 3–4 criteria to evaluate your proposed solutions. Score each solution against each criterion (1 = low, 3 = high). The highest total score indicates the most feasible solution.

Possible Criteria 评估标准例子: Time 时间考量 Manpower 人力 Cost 成本考量 Public Perception 公众认知 Sustainability 可持续性 Impact 影响力
Solutions 解决方法 Total 总评分
