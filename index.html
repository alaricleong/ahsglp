Step 2: Solution

Generate, evaluate and select your best solution

PHASE 2

5 Generate Solution Ideas / 提出解决方法

Compare and explain existing measures or solutions in both cities. Then come up with ≥3 new innovative solutions.

6 Identify Criteria & Evaluate Solutions / 设立标准并评估解决方法

Identify 3–4 criteria to evaluate your proposed solutions. Score each solution against each criterion (1 = low, 3 = high). The highest total score is your best solution.

设立标准 (3–4) 来评估自己提议的解决方法。

💡 Possible criteria / 评估标准的例子：

Time consideration 时间考量 · Manpower consideration 人力 · Cost consideration 成本考量 · Public perception 公众认知和反应
